HOW WE ARE

Why Choose Aarogya healthcare?

Aarogya Hospital is a compact, 100 bedded Multi-speciality Hospital & IVF Centre in Vaishali, Ghaziabad and 35 Multi-speciality Hospital bedded in Chitra Vihar, Delhi founded by Padamshree Awardee Late Dr. K.P.S Varma an eminent internationally acclaimed pediatrician with over 55 years of clinical knowledge who was honorary physician to the president of India for over 40 years. Started in 2003, the hospital has ensured Homely Care, comfort, Healing through compassion and best of clinical expertise to its patients from the last 19 years. Accredited with the prestigious NABH, Aarogya Hospital is known for its quality, commitment and state of the art World class facilities at affordable cost for a common man to ensure equity in healthcare industry throughout Delhi-NCR.

ABOUT AAROGYA HOSPITAL

AAROGYA HOSPITAL aims to provide whole spectrum of excellent care to each one with a human touch at an affordable price using the latest available medical knowledge & equipments without compromising the quality. A 10 bedded ICU with comprehensive centralized monitoring, advanced ventilator support & dedicated team of doctors & Para medics with facility of ABG, Pacemaker & advanced critical care. A 8 bedded Neonatal Nursery & Neonatal ICU to take care of precious ones during their hour’s of need. A four bedded Labor Delivery Suit with state of the art facilities like Cardiotocograph (Fetal CTG), Maternal & Fetal monitoring, luxurious birthing beds & pleasant & caring staff to encourage the natural process of birthing. Three Operating Rooms with laminar flow, hepafilters & modular OT to make any surgery even safer. Low flow Anesthesia gas delivery System & monitoring for neonate & adults along with facility for Invasive blood pressure monitoring , exhaled gas CO2 monitoring and critical care monitoring. Latest C-Arm for Urology, Ortho, Gastro & Neurosurgery cases. Latest laparoscopic equipment with HD vision for better sharper images & along with facilities like vessel sealing system. With the state of art medical equipments of the latest generation and most sophisticated infrastructure, backed by eminent doctors and well trained paramedical staffs, to make it a center of excellence in minimal invasive surgery.
